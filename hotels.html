Hotel Information

The Saint Paul Hotel

350 N Market St, Saint Paul, Minnesota

Rooms are available at a discounted rate at the Saint Paul Hotel - $109 per night. Book online at www.saintpaulhotel.com and use code 191109CULV for the group code or reserve by phone at 800-292-9292 and ask for the Bothe/Culverhouse Wedding rate. A shuttle to The Happy Gnome will be offered. This hotel DOES NOT have a pool.

Holiday Inn - Downtown St. Paul

175 W 7th St, Saint Paul, Minnesota

Holiday Inn won't block rooms at this location, but they offer a 10% discount if you ask for the Bothe/Culverhouse Wedding rate. This hotel is approximately 10 minutes from The Happy Gnome and right across the street from the Hampton. It DOES NOT have a pool. Book here.

Best Western - Como Park

1010 Bandana Blvd W, Saint Paul, Minnesota

Many of the Foley/Culverhouse family are staying here. You can book at a discount if you ask for the "multiple night" discount. This hotel has a pool. Book here.

About This Website

I bet most of you think this website was prompted by Lindsay. You are, in fact, incorrect. Early on, before even properly engaged, Tim said he wanted a wedding website. Lindsay said, "noooo, we don't have a cute enough 'how we met' story! If you insist, I suppose we could put when we first knew we were in love." Tim then shared that when he first knew was when Lindsay peed on the side of the road on their first trip (/second date) to California. At that moment, Lindsay knew that she definitely did NOT want a website - but, seeing as she has agreed to support Tim in all his pursuits, this website has been born. Website created by Tim, because The Knot just wasn't good enough.
