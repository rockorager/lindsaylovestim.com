About This Website

I bet most of you think this website was prompted by Lindsay. You are, in fact, incorrect. Early on, before even properly engaged, Tim said he wanted a wedding website. Lindsay said, "noooo, we don't have a cute enough 'how we met' story! If you insist, I suppose we could put when we first knew we were in love." Tim then shared that when he first knew was when Lindsay peed on the side of the road on their first trip (/second date) to California. At that moment, Lindsay knew that she definitely did NOT want a website - but, seeing as she has agreed to support Tim in all his pursuits, this website has been born. Website created by Tim, because The Knot just wasn't good enough.
